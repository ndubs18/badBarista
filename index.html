What We Offer

Think of your Coffee Card as a form of business card for your coffee order. When someone asks for your business info, you give them your business card. The same concept applies to your coffee orders. If someone asks for your coffee order, give them your personalized Coffee Card.

Coffee Cards contain all of the information necessary to ensure that you get your desired cup of coffee, every time. When ordering, simply hand the card to the barista, and from there your barista will be able to input all of the data into the computer without any confusions about your name or the details of your order.

About Us

Bad Barista is the result of an idea that emerged during a passionate conversation between two coffee lover's usual coffee orders. When we order our usual, we expect our usual. But unfortunately, sometimes our usual orders turn out a bit unusual. From correct spelling of your name, to the smallest detail in your drink creation; You name it, the baristas can mess it up.

So, to prevent this from happening, we came up with the idea of business cards for your coffee order. We call these cards Coffee Cards.
